### Featured Application Description

Breaking News for Windows Phone


### Detailed Description

Breaking News is the fastest and cleanest way to read BreakingNews.com on your Windows Phone. This application allows you to fetch the latest news updates, most popular updates, and posts for individual stories from BreakingNews.com and visit the links in Internet Explorer. You can also view pictures, pin stories to your home screen, and receive background notifications for developing stories.

This application uses the BreakingNews.com website but is not endorsed or certified by NBC News Digital.
